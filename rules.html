Server Rules

Please read and follow all rules to keep Fable SMP a great place for everyone.

1

Be Respectful

Treat all players and staff with respect. No harassment, hate speech, discrimination, or toxic behavior of any kind.

2

No Cheating or Hacking

No hacked clients, x-ray, auto-clickers, macros, or any form of unfair advantage. This includes exploiting bugs.

3

No Griefing

Do not destroy, modify, or steal from other players' builds or items without their explicit permission.

4

No Spamming

Avoid spamming chat, commands, or any form of disruption. Keep chat clean and appropriate.

5

No Advertising

Do not advertise other servers, websites, or services. This includes DMs to other players.

6

Follow Staff Directions

Staff decisions are final. If you have an issue, use the proper appeals process through Discord or the contact page.

7

No Inappropriate Builds

Keep all builds and skins appropriate. NSFW or offensive content will be removed and may result in a ban.

8

PvP Rules

PvP is only allowed in designated areas unless both players agree. No spawn killing or camping.

9

AFK Farming

AFK machines and farms are allowed but must not cause server lag. Staff reserve the right to remove them.

10

Have Fun!

The most important rule. We're all here to enjoy Minecraft together. Be a good community member!

Agree to the Rules?
